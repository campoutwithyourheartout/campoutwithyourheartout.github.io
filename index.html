Hayley

Hello, I’m Hayley. I’m a laid back introvert that lives in Pleasant Hill, CA with two roommates, 2 dogs, Villa and Bailey, and my two kitties, Munchies and Cannoli. I was born and raised in California, where I love the winters, and dread the summers. I’ve been working as a medical alert dog trainer for Dogs4Diabetics for a little over a year, and I hope to be there for many years to come. Outside of working, I enjoy vegging on the couch with my 2 dogs, travelling, hiking, camping, and finding good food to eat.

I met Dayvon while playing World of Warcraft. I was spamming general chat with a guild spam message and he happened to whisper me. We had been looking for a hunter to join our raid, and it was between him and another hunter. Even though he did worse in his trial run with us, our guild leader chose him because he could sing.
